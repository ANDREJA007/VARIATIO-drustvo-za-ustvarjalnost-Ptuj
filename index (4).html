Dobrodošli v VARIATIO

VARIATIO smo ustanovili z namenom ustvarjalnosti na številnih področjih, kjer imamo odlična znanja in izkušnje: organizacija Masterclass for Opera Singers Medea Iassonidi Ptuj z mednarodno zasedbo solistov v Minoritskem samostanu na Ptuju; klasični koncerti od opere do muzikala in še več. Posebna strast in poslanstvo pa sta kulinarika, vina in enologija. Pod blagovno znamko JEREBICA predstavljamo marmelade, vložnine in žganja.

Welcome to VARIATIO

VARIATIO was founded to foster creativity in various fields where we have outstanding expertise and experience: organizing the Masterclass for Opera Singers with Medea Iassonidi in Ptuj, featuring an international cast of soloists at the Minorite Monastery in Ptuj; classical concerts from opera to musical theatre and more. Our special passion and mission are gastronomy, wine, and oenology. Under the JEREBICA brand, we present jams, preserves, and spirits.
